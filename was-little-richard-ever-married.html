    While there are tons of musicians in the world who are talented singer-songwriters and can play an instrument or several like nobody's business, few of them can combine all of those talents with a passion for showmanship. Little Richard was one of those larger-than-life personalities who just could not be replicated.

    After his recent passing, fans of the singer are taking a closer look at his life. One question that keeps popping up is if he was ever married.
