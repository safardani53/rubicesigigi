    A PLAYBOY playmate on holiday on the Spanish island of Majorca was kicked out of the vegetable department of a supermarket because she was showing too much skin.

    German Tanja Brockmann, 29, said she got told to leave an 'Eroski' supermarket close to the beach in the Balearic holiday resort of El Arenal.

    The playmate, who lives in the German city of Cologne, said she was shopping for vegetables when she was approached by supermarket employees.

    As the weather outside was a sunny 36C Brockmann was wearing only hotpants and a tiny black top showing off her cleavage and toned stomach.

    Brockmann said: "I had the shopping cart nearly full when suddenly a staff member came to me.

    "In Spanish and then in English she said 'put your shirt on, put your shirt on' over and over again and increasingly louder."

    Yet as Brockmann had no shirt with her she could not oblige.

    Brockmann said: "A lot of people leave their stomach uncovered, I did not think of it when I went out like this.

    "I just wanted to go to a supermarket, not into a church."

    She claims she was forcefully escorted outside by the woman - who grabbed her by her arm.
