    IT'S fair to say Stacey Solomon and Joe Swash are the UK's favourite showbiz couple.

    The happy couple are forever expanding their growing brood, and are also happily married.

    When did Stacey Solomon and Joe Swash get married?

    Stacey Solomon and Joe Swash tied the knot on July 24, 2022 at their home Pickle Cottage.  

    A source said at the time: "Stacey and Joe had the best day of their lives.

    "It was exactly everything they had hoped for, the weather was glorious, and they had a joyous day with their children and family.

    "It was also important to Stacey for her day to uphold her strong Jewish values, as well as be a modern and fun day for everyone."

    In November 2019, the pair drove fans wild when they posted a picture of themselves on holiday in matching outfits, sparking wedding rumours.

    However they were quick to deny this in an Instagram video saying: "We just need to clear this up because I didn't realise while we were away.

    "Loads of people have messaged and commented saying they thought we had got married. We didn't get married.

    "We wouldn't secretly get married anyway because I'd be so over the moon about it I'd tell everyone."

    When did Stacey Solomon and Joe Swash start dating?

    Joe and Stacey have been together since 2016 but kept their relationship super quiet at first.

    However they soon opened up about their romance as fans watched them move in together and get to know each other's kids.

    The pair first sparked dating rumours in January 2016 when Stacey posted a snap of herself on a spa weekend stating that she felt like a "new woman".

    Then just days later she was spotted arm-in-arm with Joe in Essex.

    That same month, Stacey went Instagram official as she shared a snap of her new boyfriend with some flowers.

    And in May, 2019, Stacey seemingly hinted that she had tied the knot with her long-time beau in secret.

    While appearing on Loose Women, the former X Factor contestant went on to refer to Joe as her "husband".

    How many kids do Stacey Solomon and Joe Swash have?

    The pair have two children together - their son Rex Toby Francis Swash, who was born on May 23, 2019, and their daughter Rose Opal Esme Solomon-Swash, who was born on October 4, 2021.

    Stacey had her first son Zachary, whose dad is Dean Cox, as a young mother at 17.

    Her second son Leighton was born four years later, whose father is Aaron Barnham.
