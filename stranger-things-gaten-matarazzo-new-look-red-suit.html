    STRANGER Things star Gaten Matarazzo has debuted a bold new look at a red carpet event in Los Angeles.

    On Thursday, Gaten, 21, donned a bright red suit at the Netflix screening of May December, starring actresses Natalie Portman and Julianne Moore. 

    The star-studded event was held at the Academy Museum of Motion Pictures in Los Angeles, California. 

    While posing on the red carpet, the Stranger Things star modeled a printed Kidsupper Fall 2023 corduroy suit.  

    He paired the double-breasted suit with a white shirt and matching sneakers.

    The white Comedie De Kidsuper print on the jacket flowed to his red pants and lined up to the sides. 

    More On Stranger Things

    Gaten smiled at the camera as he had on thick black-rimmed glasses on his face. 

    The former child actor began his career on the Broadway stage as Benjamin in Priscilla, Queen of the Desert, and landed the role of Gavroche in Les Misérables.

    He grew up in front of the cameras as his Netflix horror/drama gained popularity and is heading towards its final fifth season.

    THE FINAL SEASON

    Viewers have become hooked over the years watching Gaten grow into his character, Dustin Henderson.

    Most read in Entertainment

    In early November, the @strangerwriters account on X, formerly known as Twitter, shared a first look at the script from the very first episode of the last season.

    The script read: “DARKNESS. The sound of COLD WIND. GROANING TREES. And…A CHILD’S VOICE.

    "Singing a familiar song:”

    Fans went wild as they jumped to the comments section as they cheered for the sneak peek.

    One fan said: "Season 5 is going to be so good. I cannot wait."

    The fourth season left fans on a cliffhanger with Dustin and his friends witnessing the town of Hawkins becoming devoured by the Upside Down.

    THE DELAYS

    Production on the final season of Stranger Things became delayed due to the Hollywood strikes.

    The strikes stemmed from the labor dispute between The Screen Actors Guild (SAG), the Alliance of Motion Picture and Television Producers (AMPTP), and the Writers Guild of America. 

    The upcoming season was initially slated to conclude in 2024 but now seems more likely to premiere in 2025 given the delays.

    After 148 days of work stoppage, the Writers Guild reached a deal with the AMPT and returned to work on Wednesday, September 27.

    On Wednesday, November 8, SAG-AFTRA reached a tentative deal with the studios on a new contract and ended its strike after 118 days.

    Stranger Things, co-starring Winona Ryder, 52, David Harbour, 48, and Millie Bobby Brown, 19, first premiered in July 2016 on the streaming site.
