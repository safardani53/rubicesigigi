    A VIBRATOR encrusted with 2,000 rare diamonds and sapphires - and boasting a built-in pearl necklace - can be yours for £1.3million.

    The world’s most expensive sex toy has taken Australian jewellery designer Colin Burn 15 years to craft.

    Mr Burn has christened the bejewelled vibe Pearl Royale to evoke “a very royal experience” and promises it will deliver a “million dollar orgasm”.

    He told the New York Post: “My inspiration was to create something that shows the value people place on sex.

    “[People] spend lots of money for the experiences of owning Maseratis and the like. But would they pay for this?”

    Weighing a little under a stone, the saucy sceptre is fitted with royal-blue sapphires and “very rare” pink diamonds.
