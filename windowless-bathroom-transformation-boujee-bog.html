    A HOMEMAKER has shared how she turned her windowless bathroom into a “boujee bog” with some panelling and stick on tiles.

    Kayla Reynolds, who runs the social media brand @thereynoldsresidence, prides herself on finding and creating “affordable home decor”.

    She regularly shares projects on her social media, with some videos totting up one million views.

    Her most recent one was turning a small and windowless toilet into a “boujee bog”.

    The caption of the video read: “If you’ve got a small, windowless downstairs loo, don’t despair!

    “You can still add character with a bit of panelling, some stick on tiles and floor vinyl for a totally affordable glow up.”

    read more on DIY

    Kayla created the panelling herself using a woodcutter in her garden and stuck the panels with a wood adhesive. 

    She went over her walls in a neutral grey paint and replaced the floor with patterned vinyl.

    People flocked to the comments to weigh in on the transformation. 

    Some dubbed the new look “amazing” and "beautiful" .

    Most read in Fabulous

    Others were shocked at “how much panelling can change the room”.

    Fellow homemakers wanting to recreate the look had plenty of questions.

    One asked: “What colour is the paint please? 

    “It's unreal.”

    Kayla replied that she used Slaked Lime Mid for the upper part of the wall and Slaked Lime Deep for the bottom.

    Both paints are by Little Greene.

    She also revealed that the vinyl flooring was from a local flooring shop.

    Another person asked: “Did you have to take the toilet off to go behind it?
