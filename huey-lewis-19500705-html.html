    Hugh Anthony Cregg III, known by his stage name Huey Lewis, is an American singer, songwriter and actor. Lewis sang and played harmonica for his band, Huey Lewis and the News, in addition to writing or co-writing many of the band's songs. The group is known for its third and best-selling, Sports, and its contribution to the soundtrack of the 1985 film Back to the Future. Lewis performed with the group Clover from 1972 to 1979.
