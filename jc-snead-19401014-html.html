Snead, who likes people to call him by his name. Buffer, Carlyle, was born in Hot Springs, Virginia, where his father worked at The Homestead complex. He attended East Tennessee State University in Johnson City, Tennessee, where he was a member of the Sigma Phi Epsilon fraternity. He played professional baseball on the Washington Senators ranch before becoming a professional golfer in 1964. He joined the PGA Tour in 1968. Snead has won eight tournaments in the PGA Tour, four Champions Tours and one international tournament. He was a member of the 1971, 1973 and 1975 Ryder Cup teams.
