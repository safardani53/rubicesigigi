    DORITOS fans are mourning the abruptly discontinued snack that had an "eerily" accurate flavour.

    The All-Nighter Cheeseburger were available on shelves from 2009-2010.

    The Discontinued Foods page on Twitter posted the snap which left hundreds of fans nostalgic at the chip flavour.

    They wrote: "All-Nighter Cheeseburger Doritos (2009-circa 2010): Part of the "Late Night" Doritos series that, through the power of food science, were made to taste exactly like a fast food cheeseburger.

    "Including subtle flavor layers of mustard, seasoned beef, pickles, cheese, and lettuce," they added.

    Doritos fans across the platform left their comments and reminisced the "pretty good" chip.

    One user added: "What the hell these sound like absolute fire. How dare they take them away".
