    Career

    Williamson previously worked as a senior sculptor for Madam Tussauds in London, where he conducted sculpture sittings with many notable contemporary people and celebrities, including Sophie, Countess of Wessex, Chinese leader Jiang Zemin, Sting, Little Richard, Eric Clapton, Bette Midler, Tony Bennett, Shirley MacLaine, Morgan Freeman, Michael Caine, Pavarotti, Australian singer Kylie Minogue, and Bollywood actor Amitabh Bachchan.

    Williamson was requested in 2001 to educate a group of Ecuadorian artists in portraiture, molding, wax casting, coloring, and hair insertion for the creation of their Historical Museum at Quito’s Metropolitan Cultural Center. In 2014, Williamson sculpted a twice-life-size bust of Professor Kin Yon Tin, one of the founders of Fudan University’s Civil Engineering Department in China. The bust was presented on October 21, 2016, in Wuhan, China.
