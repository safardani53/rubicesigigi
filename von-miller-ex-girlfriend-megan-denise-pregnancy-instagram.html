    Who is Megan Denise?

    Megan Denise is a former stripper at Girl Collection Club in Las Vegas, owned by boxing champ Floyd Mayweather.

    Denise worked at the club in 2017 for nearly a year, with her Instagram account taking off shortly after.

    She began modeling on the app while also starting her own clothing company by the same name.

    At one point, her Instagram bio said she was a licensed medical esthetician, a certified laser specialist, and a cryoskin specialist at Cherry Medical Aesthetics in Denver, Colorado, but at this time, it is unclear if she is still active in those roles.
