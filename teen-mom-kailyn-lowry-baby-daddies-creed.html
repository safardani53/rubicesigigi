    TEEN Mom's Kailyn Lowry has seven children and four baby daddies in total.

    Kailyn was cast in the second season of MTV’s 16 and Pregnant and became even more popular in its spin-off series Teen Mom.

    Jo Rivera

    The reality star’s first child came from a relationship with her boyfriend Jo Rivera.

    She gave birth in January 2010 to her son, Isaac Elliott Rivera.

    The father was just 18 when his son was born.

    Jo is married to Vee Torres and the couple has a 4-year-old daughter.

    read more on Kailyn Lowry

    Javi Marroquin

    Kailyn married Javi Marroquin, a member of the US Air Force, in September 2012 while she was pregnant with her second child.

    In 2013, she gave birth to Lincoln Marshall Marroquin.

    The two began having marriage issues and appeared on Marriage Boot Camp, but ultimately divorced in 2015.

    They agreed to share custody of their son, and Kailyn denied rumors that their marriage had ended due to infidelity.

    Chris Lopez

    Kailyn began dating her friend Chris Lopez and became pregnant with her third child, Lux Russell Lowry, who was born in August 2017.

    Fans celebrated in the summer of 2020 when it was revealed that the Teen Mom 2 star had given birth to her and Lopez's second son, Creed.

    Kailyn and Chris have had a turbulent on-and-off relationship and are currently on the outs.

    During an Instagram Live Chris revealed he was banned from her home birth and that he wasn't sure when was the last time he saw Lux.

    He recently said it's "none of his business" if she's dating a "new man."

    Who are Kailyn Lowry’s kids?

    Kailyn is mom to Isaac, 13, Lincoln, 9, and Lux, 6, Creed, 3, and Rio, 1.

    Kailyn welcomed Rio with her current boyfriend, Elijah Scott.

    On November 3, 2023, the U.S. Sun exclusively reported that Kailyn had given birth to twins, a boy and a girl, with Elijah.

    The arrival of the twins comes one week after Kailyn confirmed she was pregnant with two children.

    When did Kailyn Lowry announce she was pregnant again?

    On an episode of her podcast Barely Famous on Friday, October 28, 2023, Kailyn announced that she was expecting twins with her boyfriend Elijah Scott.
