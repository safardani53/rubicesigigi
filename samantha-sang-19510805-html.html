    Cheryl Lau Sang, known as Samantha Sang, is an Australian singer. She first received national recognition in Australia in 1967. , after releasing the top 10 single "You Made Me What I Am". In 1969, Sang moved to the UK where she worked with Bee Gees, before returning to Australia in 1975. She reconnected with Bee Gees in 1977 and succeeded internationally with the song "Emotion", reaching third place on the Billboard Hot 100, number two in Australia and number eleven in the UK. The single's parent album, Emotion, entered the Billboard 200 top thirty and included two other singles.
