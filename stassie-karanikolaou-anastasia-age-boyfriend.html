    Who is Stassie Karanikolaou?

    Born on June 9, 1997, Stassie Karanikolaou - real name Anastasia - is a social media influencer and the longtime best friend of Kylie Jenner.

    With over 10.8million followers on Instagram, Stassie is no stranger to the limelight.

    The starlet often joins Kylie and the Kardashians for family events and parties and is known to have a wide array of celebrity friends herself.

    In March of 2022, Stassie and fellow influencer Zack Bia joined forces in launching Sunny Vodka.
