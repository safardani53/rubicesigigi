    Howie Mandel is a comedian and one of the judges on ‘America’s Got  Talent.’ Check out photos of him here!

    Howie Mandel is one of the most-recognized comedians across North America. The Canadian superstar has made his mark across the worlds of TV and comedy. His stand-up comedy career kicked off in the 1970s, and he’s made a number of comedy specials, his most recent being the upcoming ‘Howie Mandel & Friends: Don’t Sneeze On Me,’ which is expected to premiere in 2021. He’s also acted in a number of TV and movies, such as the show ‘St. Elsewhere’ and the movie ‘Little Monsters.’

    Other than comedy, Howie has made a huge impact in the game show and reality TV worlds. In 2005, he began hosting the beloved game show ‘Deal or No Deal,’ which was based on a Dutch game show. In 2010, he joined ‘America’s Got Talent’ as one of the judges, and has been a fixture on the show longer than any other judge, having been on for 12 seasons in a row.
