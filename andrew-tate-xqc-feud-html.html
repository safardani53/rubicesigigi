    16 Mar 2024, 00:00

    The heated exchange between four-time kickboxing world champion Andrew Tate and popular Twitch streamer Felix “xQc” Lengyel during a live stream has gone viral on the internet. 

    On July 14, 2022, Tate, xQc, fellow streamers Adin Ross, Trainwreck, and LosPollosTV streamed a Discord call. 

    They were having a debate on chivalry and discussed hypothetical situations where a man would have to sacrifice himself to save a woman.

    One of the main issues discussed was what would happen if the woman had more knowledge or training to resolve the situation. 

    The former Overwatch pro argued that if the woman was better equipped to handle the situation, then she should be the one to resolve it, but Tate strongly disagreed. 

    Tate argued it was still the man’s job to ensure he kept his woman safe. xQc maintained his stance and continued arguing his points until the former boxer had enough. He was livid and left the call, but not before he criticized the streamer. 

    Andrew Tate and xQc on discord call debate which led to their feud. (Source: Instagram)

    “To sit here and talk to a man who thinks he can send his chick downstairs to [expletive] engage armed men, I know I’m wasting my time,” said Tate and then left the call. 

    xQc and Andrew Tate’s Online Feud Continues

    After Tate hung up, the other streamers laughed about the arguments and continued to debate amongst themselves.

    Fans, too, were on xQc’s side, saying his arguments were logical, and criticized Tate’s arguments, saying they were poorly thought out. 

    You would think the duo’s conflict would end after the call, but on July 16, 2022, during his stream, xQc further explained why he challenged Tate. 

    During the stream, some viewers taunted the French Canadian streamer for “losing the debate,” which led him to address the online beef once more.

    He explained it was the last time he would talk about it, but either way, he knew some would understand his view and others would not. 

    “A lot of his takes are legitimately misogynistic, and they’re very anti-woman takes and behaviors, If everybody replicates those thoughts and behaviors in the public without money or status or whatever, it is complete disarray right,” said the streamer. 

    He added most people that talked to him didn’t go against his takes and just nodded their heads, but not him. 

    xQc was not going to let “some guy that’s anti-woman go on anti-woman rants on the stream.” 

    Who Is Andrew Tate?

    For the unversed, Tate is a kickboxer turned entrepreneur with a net worth in trillions. 

    Tate claimed he was the world’s first trillionaire during a previous Twitch stream with Ross. 

    But according to reports, the combined net worth of two of the world’s richest men, Elon Musk and Jeff Bezos, is only $400 billion, which is a far cry from a trillion. 

    “I was broke for a long time. I made my first million when I was, say, 27, and then I had 100 million by the time I was 31, and then I became a trillionaire recently,” said Tate. 

    However, some reports state the former kickboxer is worth $30 million. Trillionaire or not, he has been more famous in recent years for promoting the alpha male lifestyle, which often comes with controversial opinions.
