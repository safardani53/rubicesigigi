    WALMART shoppers have been thrilled as a popular snack has returned to the shelves just in time for Halloween.

    Chip lovers have proclaimed that it’s the start of the “spooky season” after the treat made a comeback.

    A TikToker (@snackgoon) couldn’t contain his enthusiasm when he discovered that Zombie Takis are back in Walmart.

    Approaching the bags of chips, he said: “No way. They’re back. I’m taking them all.”

    The short 12-second clip cut out just as the TikToker went to grab the bags of chips.

    Fellow influencers rushed to comment to share how excited they were when they spotted the snacks.

    READ MORE on Money

    One said: “I screamed when I saw them at Walmart. Everyone looked at me weird.”

    Walmart shoppers can buy a box of 46 packets of the habanero and cucumber chips for $35.60 online.

    One sharing bag of Zombie Takis will set shoppers back $12.40.

    The Zombie Takis have made a comeback after fans said they were “hurt” the snacks had been taken off the shelves.

    Most read in Money

    Barcel USA, the company behind Takis, explained that the zombie Takis are always available for a limited time around Halloween.

    The company said: “Hey!!! Zombie only haunts shelves from August until Halloween... def not discontinued.”

    Zombie Takis is not the only flavor that’s manufactured by Barcel USA.

    Other flavors include “Fuego” and the chips are described as having a taste of fire.

    Takis Blue Heat has been branded as a “sun flare for your face” while other flavors include guacamole, Nitro, and Intense Nacho.

    Takis have been a staple of the snack market since entering the US in 2004 - years after the treat was rolled out in Mexico.
