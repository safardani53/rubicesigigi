    03 Jun 2024, 00:00

    Are Madelyn Cline and Chase Stokes officially over?

    According to Deuxmoi, a completely anonymous gossip account, Cline was spotted with Zack Bia.

    In an Instagram story, Deuxmoi uploaded a screenshot of a follower's DM, including a blurry photo of Cline with a male companion.

    According to the follower, the guy "kinda looked like Zack Bia from Weho." The duo walked past the follower, who was at a car wash.

    But an hour later, Deuxmoi gave an update saying that Bia and Cline were dating. 

    Deuxmoi Confirms Zack Bia And Madelyn Cline Are Dating (Source: Deuxmoi’s Instagram)

    It is unconfirmed, but Deuxmoi has a track record of being relatively accurate. The IG account also stated the information came from a reliable source.

    Who is Zack Bia?

    And for those wondering, Zack Bia was an ordinary student at the University of Southern California.

    But at night, he worked as the customer acquisition and creative director for H.Wood Group. 

    He slowly but surely became the next “It Boy,” from sitting courtside at a Lakers Game with Odell Beckham Jr to having dinner with world-famous DJ Diplo.

    According to GQ, Bia is the “Messi of clubbing,” and rolling with him gets you the luxurious treatment.

    He even earned a co-sign from Drake after working with him for six months and proved that he was valuable and not in it for clout.

    Bia has appeared in Drake’s music video and was name-dropped in ‘No Stylist.’  

    As he networked, he realized that he needed to start a business.

    So in 2018, Bia and his business partner Jonah Levine launched Psychworld, a streetwear fashion brand.

    Brands like Hypebeast and his friend Drake promoted the brand. 

    According to the entrepreneur, this resulted in $100,000 in sales in over an hour.

    After conquering the fashion world, he tried his hand in music and started as a DJ at fashion weeks and parties. 

    Today, the multi-hyphenate entrepreneur is a source of inspiration for Gen-Z creatives. 

    Madelyn Cline and Chase Stokes Relationship

    Before the Outer Banks actress and Bia were spotted together, Cline was in a high-profile relationship with Stokes.

    The speculations of their relationship first started in April last year when fans noticed that Cline and Stokes had the same background for their interview with E! News. 

    But two months later, the actors confirmed they were dating when they went public with their relationship on Instagram. 

    Then in November, Cline opened up about her relationship with Stokes and why they kept it private initially. 

    She explained that they just wanted to enjoy their relationship privately while it was new and added that she was in love with her boyfriend.  

    Everything was going great between the two until this November, when reports surfaced that the two had a break up. 

    According to People, a source claimed that the couple had broken up and tried to work things out privately. 

    However, shortly after, rumors stated that they were back together when a fan named Leonard Cole uploaded a photo with the duo on Instagram. 

    After that, on December 7, E! News reported that according to a source, the former couple was spotted looking “close and cozy” when having an “intimate” conversation at an after-party for the 2021 People’s Choice Awards.

    Some fans even speculated that they had never split and were still together.
