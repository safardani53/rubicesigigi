    Sofia Vergara doesn't do subtle.

    The 39-year-old Colombian beauty played a transsexual in the 2006 direct-to-video movie Grilled, and according to the actress, it wasn't much of a stretch.

    The Modern Family star adds that she's "always had plenty of gay fans," mostly because she's "voluptuous and a little over the top."
