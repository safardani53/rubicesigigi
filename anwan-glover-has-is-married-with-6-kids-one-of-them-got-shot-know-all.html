    Anwan Glover and his dating history

Glover gained a lot of fame from HBO’s series as well.

    Anwan Glover is married with kids

    Glover, who has never spoken about his girlfriends and relationships, seems to be a married guy. He has six kids altogether. He often shares photos of his kid on his Instagram but he has never posted any photos with his wife.

Thankfully, the doctors were able to save him. It seems like Glover is very attached to his son. During an interview, he said: “I got the call . . . and my heart dropped into my stomach.
