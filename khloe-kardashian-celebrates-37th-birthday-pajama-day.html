    KHLOE Kardashian celebrated her 37th birthday with a low-key pajama party alongside her sisters following her split with baby daddy Tristan.

    The Keeping Up With The Kardashian's star previously revealed her relaxed birthday plans as she enjoyed a pool day with her daughter True, three.

    Khloe, now 37, went for a more laidback birthday celebration this time around, despite years of over-the-top lavish parties.

    The reality star spent the evening with her sisters Kourtney and Kylie, and best friends Malika and Khadijah.

    The TV personality wore a "birthday girl" party tiara and indulged in cake as she thanked Kylie, 23, for hosting the group.

    "My pajama birthday! Thank you @kyliejenner for everything, always!!" she wrote on her Instagram stories.

    The ladies began the evening in silky nightgowns and lingerie but later moved to more comfy attire.

    They cheers'd champagne and danced outside by the pool with pizza, and later had an indoor pillow fight.

    Khloe also thanked her family, friends, and fans for their birthday love, as she wrote: "Thank you for all the beautiful birthday wishes, flowers, and love!!!

    "I can't even begin to express how blessed I feel by all of your love and support. Thank you to everyone!!!!" she gushed.

    The mother of one's birthday celebration was much understated compared to years past, when she's joined friends on lavish vacations, or held massive parties at her LA mansion.

    Khloe opted for a more casual day, following the news that she and her baby daddy Tristan Thompson had once again split ways after more cheating allegations surfaced against the athlete.

    One fan inquired about the special celebration, asking: “How's your day so far? Are we going to get bomb ass birthday photos like last year?”

    “I’m not doing anything today. I’ve just been swimming with True all morning. But we’re having a great day,” the TV star replied, as many were shocked she opted for a quiet affair.

    Despite their recent breakup, Tristan, 30, made sure to send well wishes to his baby mama, as he posted several photos with Khloe for her big day.

    "Happy birthday @khloekardashian. Thank you for being not just an amazing partner, mommy and best friend but also being the kindest, caring and most loving human being I’ve ever met," he wrote alongside the images.

    "Your love and spirit is contagious to all who’ve met you. Thank you for always being there for me and putting our family first. I love you so much. Have an amazing day…."

    However, fans were confused by the post which seemed to ignore the fact that Tristan was yet again accused of cheating on Khloe after their reconciliation during the pandemic.

    "Can't wait for the comments..." one slammed while another chimed in with, "No shame in his game. Sadness.”

    "I am confused here…" a third complained.

    Last week Tristan was seen heading into a bedroom during a Bel-Air party with three unknown women, emerging a half-hour later, looking “disheveled."
