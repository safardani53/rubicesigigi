    A COIN collector has just revealed what could take 2022 quarters from pocket change to game changers.

    Tiktok user TheCoinChannel posted a video explaining why 2022 quarters with a certain error are selling for over $400 each.

    The collector, who real first name is Eric, uses his platform to educate his over four million followers about rare coins.

    In this video, he explained why some 2022 quarters are currently selling for hundreds of dollars.

    The quarters in question feature astronaut Dr Sally Ride on their back "tails" side.

    The error comes in the form of two lines on each side of Dr Ride's face.

    Read More on Rare Coins

    But these are no ordinary lines.

    According to Eric, these lines are actually a result of the image of George Washington's face from the "heads" side being clashed into the back of the coin.

    He added: "We're about to see a lot of people making $400 on a quarter."

    Either way, now may be a good time to check and see if you have one of these quarters.

    Most read in Money

    On July 2, one of these coins sold for $410 on eBay after racking up 77 bids.

    This isn't Eric's first time talking about these exact quarters.

    The influencer mentioned in his video that he talked about the quarters earlier this year when they were selling for around $63 each.

    Eric predicted that the coin's value would increase, and he was right.

    This also isn't his first time talking about errors in 2022 quarters, as one of his others videos features a quarter with a crack in it.

    This quarter sold for even more at around $700.

    How to know if your coin is valuable

    Checking your spare change for any rare nickels, half dollars, dimes, and others might just lead to the discovery of a rare coin that could sell for thousands.

    Typically with coin collecting, the most valuable coins are either very old or contain mintage errors.

    This is because there are usually fewer of them available, making them harder to find and more valuable to collectors.

    If you're unsure how much a coin you have can sell for, a quick way to check its value is by searching the coin on eBay to see what it or similar coins have sold for in the past.

    Other vendors and appraisers, like Coinappraiser and USA Coin Bank, can also help you learn about and sell your rare coins.
