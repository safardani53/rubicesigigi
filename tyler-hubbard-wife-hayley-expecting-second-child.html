    08 Oct 2024, 00:00

    Baby No. 2 is officially on the way! Tyler Hubbard and his wife, Hayley Hubbard, are expecting their second child, their rep confirms to Us Weekly.

    Tyler lovingly kissed Hayley’s baby bump on the red carpet at Clive Davis‘ annual pre-Grammy party on Saturday, February 9.

    Their happy news comes just five months after the country crooner, 32, opened up exclusively to Us Weekly about the couple’s hope to expand their brood. “You never know!” Tyler gushed to Us in September 2018. “We’re gonna steadily be working on it and see what happens.”

    The Florida Georgia Line member and his wife welcomed their first child — a daughter named Olivia Rose — in December 2017. Tyler announced the arrival of their baby girl in a sweet Instagram post on Christmas Eve.

    “I had no idea I could love somebody as much as I love @hayley_hubbard but last night God gave us Olivia Rose and melted our hearts,” he captioned a photo that showed him doting on Hayley as she cradled their newborn. “We love this little girl more than words can describe and it hasn’t even been 24 hours yet. Our life has been forever been changed for the better.”

    Tyler added: “I can’t wait to share more photos with y’all. I’ll try to contain myself from being the annoying parent that post 50 pics a day but I’ve already taken over 100 so forgive me.”

    Becoming parents hasn’t stopped the pair, who tied the knot in July 2015, from enjoying their time together. Hayley told Us in September that date nights for her and her hubby “could be anything from just being at the house to cooking dinner, to being in PJs, to this, I guess, getting all dressed up and having a fun night out.”

    Tyler and Hayley also dished on their philanthropic efforts. “We love giving back, we love anything that has to do with the great purpose of helping people that need help,” the “This Is How We Roll” singer explained to Us at the time. “Haiti is especially close to our heart.”
