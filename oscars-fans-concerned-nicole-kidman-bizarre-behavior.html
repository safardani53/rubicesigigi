    OSCARS fans have taken to social media to express their concern for actress Nicole Kidman after her behavior on the award show's red carpet.

    Nicole sparked some conversation among fans online after she displayed bizarre behavior in shocking new snaps that were taken on the 2023 Oscars red carpet.

    The 55-year-old Oscar-winner stunned on this year's award show's red carpet, sporting a black sequin dress with a thigh-high split and one long sleeve.

    Two huge black-grey roses adorned the dress, one on the sleeveless part while the other on her waist, just above her slit.

    Nicole paired the look with simple black ankle-strapped high heels, pulling the ensemble together with natural makeup and a tousled hairstyle, making her look effortless.

    Though Nicole looked stunning in her outfit on the red carpet, her sleek look hasn't really been the topic of discussion online.

    Read more on Nicole Kidman

    More fans have flooded Twitter with tweets about the way Nicole was posing and moving for the cameras than how she was dressed for the ceremony.

    "I want an order of whatever Nicole Kidman has had #Oscars#Oscars2023," one fan tweeted, implying that she might be under the influence.

    Another person blatantly asked on Twitter: "Is Nicole Kidman drunk? #Oscars."

    "Seems that way to me, all her weird posing," a person replied on the platform.

    A fourth expressed in a tweet: "Is Nicole okay?? Serious question. She seems out of it."

    NICOLE'S NOMINATIONS

    Nicole isn't nominated for an Oscar this year; however, she was last year.

    In 2022, she was nominated for Best Actress for her role of Lucille Ball in Being the Ricardos.

    She lost out on the gong to Jessica Chastain who won for her role in The Eyes of Tammy Faye.

    Steven Spielberg is nominated for directing for The Fabelmans, going up against Everything Everywhere All At Once's duo for that award (as well as 10 other noms).

    Lady Gaga is nominated for best song as are Tems, Rihanna, Ryan Coogler, and Ludwig Goransson for Lift Me Up from Wakanda Forever.

    The celebrities nominated for the award show include the crooning Austin Bulter, Brendan Fraser, Ke Huy Quan, Ana de Armas, Michelle Yeoh, and Angela Bassett for acting categories.

    Throughout her entire career, Nicole has been nominated four times for Best Actress at the Oscars.

    The movies that yielded her the Oscar nod, in addition to Being the Ricardos, include Moulin Rouge, The Hours, and The Killing of a Sacred Deer.
