    A BEAUTY fan has warned others not to get Botox to fix a gummy smile after she was left looking like a monster.

    Chloe, from the US, revealed she would never get the procedure done again on TikTok.

    In the clip, the beauty fan said: "Things that I would never, ever, ever again for any reason, under any circumstance, ever, ever get done to my face again.

    "Gummy smile Botox, oh my god."

    She warned people that the pictures of her with Botox in her upper lip were enough by themselves to put them off getting it done.

    Before that, she showed her smile before getting Botox, and in hindsight realised it wasn't even a gummy smile.

    But the next picture showed her smile with Botox, and it was a sight to see.

    To top it off, it took FIVE months for the Botox to wear off.

    Her top lip had completely frozen, and she added: "I look like literally a monster."

    Chloe revealed that she had gone to the clinic to get filler dissolved from her face when the clinic offered her gummy smile Botox.
