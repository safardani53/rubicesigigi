    I'm still a teen and earn $2,000 a month with my side hustle - it only costs you $45 to get started

    25 Apr 2024, 00:00

    IF you're looking to add an extra $2,000 to your monthly income, one teen has a side hustle you can start for just $45.

    Mark, who uses the TikTok handle @financeunfolded, shared his lucrative side hustle in a recent video.

    Mark captioned his TikTok video: "one of the best side hustles to start with a friend if you like nice cars."

    Throughout the video, he reveals that he makes his money washing luxury cars.

    He claims it to be one of the best side hustles for teens.

    To start the gig, Mark says he purchased ingredients that cost him about $45, including a special car soap.

    Next, Mark says it's helpful to have a dad with a hose to be able to wash the cars.

    He then took the third step of logging onto Facebook to find exotic car groups.

    "These kinds of cars can't be taken through car washes," he says.

    "Offer to go to their house and wash it [the car] by hand."

    Mark says it typically takes one hour to wash a car.

    The industry rate for sports cars ranges from $100 to $150 per hour.

    He says that by charging $100 per hour and washing five cars per week, he makes an extra $2,000 per month.

    The video has been viewed more than 1.6million times.

    Some were skeptical of Mark's side hustle, while others thought it was a great idea.
