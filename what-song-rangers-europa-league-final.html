    What song do Rangers fans sing during Europa League games?

    RANGERS are set to take on Eintracht Frankfurt TONIGHT in the Europa League final - which gives the fans the chance to test out their vocal cords. The Scottish giants arguably have some of the most passionate supports in world football.

    16 May 2024, 00:00

    RANGERS are set to take on Eintracht Frankfurt TONIGHT in the Europa League final - which gives the fans the chance to test out their vocal cords.

    The Scottish giants arguably have some of the most passionate supports in world football.

    And they tend to make their presence felt with a spine-tingling song most notably heard throughout their Europa League campaign.

    But what is the name of the song and why is so special to the Gers?

    What is the name of the song?

    During Rangers' European games this season, the song fans bellow at the top of their lungs goes by the name of "I'm feeling it".

    The song was released by Sunset Bros and Marc MaCabe in 2017 and has quickly become the national anthem at Ibrox.

    Read More on Rangers

    Gers stars were recorded singing and dancing "I'm feeling it" after they all-but-sealed the Scottish Premiership title last season with a crucial 3-0 win over St. Mirren.

    And the song went viral once again when it was sung by 50,000 Rangers fans after a huge win over Celtic last August.

    What European games has it been sung at?

    "I'm feeling it" was belted out by Rangers fans after their round of 32 triumph over Borussia Dortmund.

    And when the Scottish heavyweights knocked Red Star Belgrade out of the Europa League in the last 16.

    Most read in Europa League

    But none of the above match the way it was sung after the rollercoaster win against Braga in the quarter-finals.

    The noise of French referee Francois Letexier full time whistle was quickly replaced by the song being recited at a high volume.

    What has been said about the song?

    DJ Adriano Mercurio - one half of the Sunset bros, admitted that he was blown away and is now a Light Blues supporter.

    Mercurio said: “We don’t think there is a perfect way to describe it but with everything going on in the world, it was heart-warming to see so many people side by side screaming it at the top of their lungs.

    “It’s what football fans deserve. We will never forget it.

    “We’ve been lucky enough to see a few live matches around the world but after witnessing the atmosphere at the Rangers v Celtic we 100 per cent want to go the minute we are out of lockdown and can travel.

    “We woke up one morning to numerous tags and text messages from friends and fans from all over the world. It was such a surreal feeling to see the happiness it brought the squad.

    The duo behind Rangers' anthem promised to one day perform "I'm feeling it" live.
