    MARIE Kondo is a Japanese organizing consultant, author and television show host.

    Kondo, aka Konmari, has written four books on organizing, collectively selling millions of copies worldwide.

    Who is Marie Kondo's husband Takumi Kawahara?

    Since 2012, Kondo has been married to Takumi Kawahara, 36.

    Born in July 1984 in Osaka, Japan, Kawahara is an established Japanese producer, businessman and CEO of KonMari Media Inc.

    Before KonMari Media, Kawahara worked as a corporate HR consultant and strategist for Achievement Co in Tokyo.

    After marrying Kondo in 2012, he left his job to join his wife as her company manager.

    The couple launched KonMari Media Inc in 2015.

    Kawahara accompanies his wife frequently in her interviews, helping her handle her book deals and occasionally working as Kondo's photographer.

    In 2018, the couple signed a deal with Netflix, which saw Kawahara become the executive producer of their series Tidying Up with Marie Kondo.

    The Netflix deal came after the success of Kondo's book, The Life-Changing Magic of Tidying Up.

    Tidying Up with Marie Kondo officially premiered in January 2019.
