    Audrina Patridge, born on May 8, 1985, is an American television personality, presenter, actress and model. She rose to fame in 2006 after she was cast in the hit reality television series, ‘The Hills’, alongside Lauren Conrad, Heidi Montag and Whitney Port.

    Later that year, she competed on the eleventh season of Dancing with the Stars, and finished in seventh place. In 2011, she landed her own television series, Audrina, which documented the lives of her and her family. She’s starred in films such ‘Sorority Row’ (2009) and ‘Into the Blue 2: The Reef’ (2011), as well as ‘Honey 2’ (2011) and ‘Scary Movie 5’ (2013), as a featured character.

    Her scenes were later removed from ‘Scary Movie 5’ and did not make it to the final version. In 2014 and 2015, Audrina hosted NBC’s late-night travel show ‘1st Look’. She stars in MTV’s reboot of ‘The Hills’, titled, ‘The Hills: New Beginnings’. Audrina began dating motorcycle rider and professional BMX dirt bike rider, Corey Bohan and he proposed in November 2015. She gave birth to their daughter, Kirra on June 24, 2016. Audrina and Corey got married in November 2016. Audrina filed for divorce on September 20, 2017 and filed a restraining order against Corey. The divorce was finalized on December 20, 2018.
