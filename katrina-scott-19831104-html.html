In September 2019, Scott along with Robin Montgomery and Connie Ma won the Youth League Cup, the third consecutive victory in the United States. Scott and Montgomery followed in the footsteps of Amanda Anisimova and Coco Gauff, who had been on the winning teams in previous years. Scott made his premium Grand Slam debut at the 2020 US Open at the USTA Billie Jean King National Tennis Center in Flushing Meadow as a wildcard. Scott beat Natalia Vikhlyantseva in successive sets to win her first match and beat Amanda Anisimova before losing the second round match.
