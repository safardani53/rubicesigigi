    Brandi Glanville: Drunken Night Was A ‘Bad Choice’ But ‘It’s Not Murder’

    While Brandi, 40, admits on Twitter that she is “embarrassed” about the mother of all wardrobe malfunctions, she was quick to defend herself against attacks from Twitter users, according to the UK’s Daily Mail.

    The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills star replied to one diss by saying, “It was not a hot look I agree, bad choice and I’m not happy with myself all I can do is say sorry and check myself (and nap) :)”

    Brandi has also been slammed on Twitter for setting a bad example for her sons Mason, 10, and Jake, 5. She responded to the accusation, tweeting, “I never drink in front of them, and honestly I have a lot on my plate and needed to let my hair down. (But not my dress)”

    At least Brandi has a sense of humor about the embarrassing photos.

    Brandi Glanville Defends Herself Against Twitter Attacks About Wardrobe Malfunction

    It sounds like Brandi knows what she did was wrong, but she is still being very defensive about the attacks.

    “When ur PERFECT and make all the right decisions ALL time, and don’t gave at least a bit of cellulite on ur butt, get back to me,” she tweeted to her attackers.

    She also explained, “I got drunk with my gays, its not murder,, everyone kept sending us drinks. I was being polite,” adding, “I forgot that my every move was watched, I was letting go with my gays.”

    We don’t blame Brandi for defending herself, but she should just own up to what she did and stop making excuses.
