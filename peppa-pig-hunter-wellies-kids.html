    Hunter is selling kids Peppa Pig wellies for 30 which are perfect for jumping in muddy puddles

    HUNTER Boots have teamed up with children's favourite Peppa Pig to create an adorable collection of wellies. The limited edition wellies have been designed to celebrate &quot;the joy of muddy puddles&quot; and fans CAN'T WAIT.

    20 Feb 2024, 00:00

    HUNTER Boots have teamed up with children's favourite Peppa Pig to create an adorable collection of wellies.

    The limited edition wellies have been designed to celebrate "the joy of muddy puddles" and fans CAN'T WAIT.

    The collaboration is expected to drop on 19th July and will also feature umbrellas splashed with the iconic Peppa Pig print.

    The fun unisex designs look set to be a hit with little ones and their parents, with some yearning for the boots to be made available in adult sizes!

    One Instagram user said: "I wish you made these in adult sizes... I know some women that would love them!"

    While another shopper asked: "Serious question...are there adult sizes lol"

    Others were keen to praise the brand for the genius collaboration, saying "Omg this would be so good for my little boy, he loves Peppa Pig so much."

    Sadly, adults who want to splash around will have to stick to traditional wellingtons, as Hunter have admitted the collection is limited to kids sizes only.

    In some good news, you can pick up a set for your child from between £30 and £45.

    This isn't the first time Hunter has created a collection with a children's character in mind.

    Last year saw Hunter x Disney featuring Mary Poppins themed footwear, in time for the film's release.

    However there is something extra special about partnering with Peppa, a character who is known to love playing in the mud.

    Speaking to Footwear News in February, Alasdhair Willis, creative director at Hunter described the essence of the company as "fun, adventure and preferably a muddy puddle".

    "I can think of no better partner to bring this spirit to life than Peppa Pig.".

    The popular cartoon character is celebrating its 15th anniversary this year, with over 300 episodes aired and many more to come.

    To mark the occasion, Peppa Pig is also releasing a debut album on the same day the wellingtons drop.

    Super fans can expect all their favourite tracks from the show, as well as extended versions of songs composed by Julian Nott, such as Bing Bong Zoo, Rainbow, Rainbow and Jumping in Muddy Puddles.

    If these aren't cute enough, we recently revealed you can pick up matching mother and daughter Disney-themed pyjamas. 

    Meanwhile Kate Middleton's favourite kids brand has a 50% off sale - so your little one can dress like a royal from £22.
