    A PROFESSIONAL dog walker who spends the majority of winter outside has shared her new go-to cold-weather clothes.

    And one of the pieces is a bargain from everyone’s favourite budget store, B&M. 

    Maisie Elliot’s Thermal Brushed Long Sleeve Top is 1.0 tog and has “super soft plush lining”. 

    It will set you back just £8 from B&M but is guaranteed to keep you warm now we’re in the throes of winter. 

    Maisie took to TikTok to share what she wears to keep cosy and dry when spending muddy days outside. 

    “The rain today was awful so I bought these new thermal clothes from B&M,” the 24-year-old said. 

    read more on clothes

    “I decided to give them a go today. 

    “I’m also wearing my Acai thermal and waterproof trousers with a thermal top from B&M.”

    Maisie also wears men's thermal socks from B&M, which cost £5 for a four-pack.

    More on the expensive side, she paired her budget thermal top with a £99 pair of trousers from outdoor clothing wear brand, Acai. 

    Most read in Fabulous

    The Thermal Skinny Outdoor Trousers come in eleven colours, and are fleece-lined and waterproof. 

    According to Maisie, they keep her “bone dry even when it’s been heaving it down”. 

    Based in Scotland, she runs dog-walking business Tail Of A Time. 

    On an average day at work, she will take six pooches for a walk, meaning she spends most of the day inside. 

    During the colder months, wearing appropriate clothing is particularly important. 

    According to Acai bosses, Maisie’s trousers “are the ultimate cold weather performance legwear for women who want to keep adventuring deep into the winter months”. 

    “The form and fleece lining feels like a hug and fits like a glove,” they added.

    “They mimic the look of skinny jeans whilst providing warmth, shower resistance and superb comfort.
