    Primark is selling Disney Christmas baubles... and the Minnie Mouse ones are adorable

    ITS the most wonderful time of the year and its about to get a whole lot better thanks to Primarks Disney baubles. The budget high-street retailer is now selling Mickey Mouse, Beauty And The Beast, Frozen and Winnie The Pooh-themed Christmas decorations.

    29 May 2024, 00:00

    IT’S the most wonderful time of the year – and it’s about to get a whole lot better thanks to Primark’s Disney baubles.

    The budget high-street retailer is now selling Mickey Mouse, Beauty And The Beast, Frozen and Winnie The Pooh-themed Christmas decorations.

    A four pack comes in at a purse-friendly £4, while the special singular ones cost £5.

    The cutest item in the festive range is a pink Minnie Mouse bauble, which is decorated in silver glitter and a bow.

    Last month, we revealed Primark has launched a Harry Potter Christmas range.

    The wizard collection includes baubles emblazoned with the famous Hogwarts crest and a Platform 9 ¾ logo.

    The best Christmas 2017 decorations for your tree, table and outdoors

    The products will be released in UK stores at the end of September.

    Meanwhile, Primark sent shoppers into a frenzy in August after launching a Harry Potter range, including clothes, homeware and stationery with prices starting at just £2.

    The magical line includes fairy lights shaped like potion bottles for £8, which could also be the perfect addition to a Christmas tree.

    Primark have been smashing the themed collections in recent months, with Beauty And The Beast merchandise selling the fastest.

    The Chip mug recently caused a stampede at the stores and a £4 Chip purse sold out in hours.
