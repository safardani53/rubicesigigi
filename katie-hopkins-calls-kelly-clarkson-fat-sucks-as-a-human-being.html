    Earlier this week, in promotion of her upcoming album, Kelly Clarkson performed across the pond on The Graham Norton Show.

    She belted out a new track from "Piece By Piece," which hits stores on March 3 and which has fans of music around the world crazy excited.

    But not Katie Hopkins. This random British television personality has chosen to focus on something aside from Clarkson’s amazing personality and singing voice:

    Her figure.

    "Jesus, what happened to Kelly Clarkson? Did she eat all of her backing singers? Happily I have wide-screen," Hopkins Tweeted in response to Clarkson’s appearance with Norton.

    When a follower pointed out that Clarkson recently gave birth to a daughter named River Rose, Hopkins grew even meaner.

    "Darling, if you had a baby a year ago, that is not baby weight. It is fat. Quit calling it cute names to make yourself feel better," she replied, later adding:

    "Baby weight, puppy fat, muffin top. We’re so weakwilled we have to make up cute names for fat. Babies, puppies, muffins. Nope. Just fat love."

    Hopkins makes a living out of being a terrible person.

    Yesterday, as a way to diss Kelly Obourne’s fashion choice at the Academy Awards, Hopkins posted a photo of Troll online and wrote:

    "Nice of Kelly O. To make such an effort for Oscar night."

    Wrote the former reality star in response to Hopkins: "no 1 gives a f***about U anymore. What a sad old woman U R that it takes me to make U relevant."

    Amen, Kelly.
