    GREMLINS and Home Alone star Haviland Morris is unrecognisable 30 years after starring in the iconic 90s flicks.

    The 62-year-old actress is currently working as an estate agent for New York firm Brown Harris Stevens, flogging properties with price tags upwards of $1million.

    She hasn't completely turned her back on acting though, occasionally appearing on TV.

    The mum-of-two has a long list of credits to her name on both the big and small screen.

    Her film debut came in 1984 Daryl Hannah flick Reckless which she followed with appearances in coming of age film Sixteen Candles and Madonna's Who's That Girl.

    In 1990 she played Marla Bloodstone in Gremlins 2 and seven years later she starred as Karen Pruitt in Home Alone 3.

    Her notable telly credits include parts in Sex & the City, Law & Order, One Tree Hill and Blue Bloods.

    The role for which she is best known is probably the party-loving teen Caroline Mulford in Sixteen Candles.

    In the flick, her relationship with boyfriend Jake becomes increasingly strained as he grows tired of her wild ways.
