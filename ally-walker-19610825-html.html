    Allene Damian "Ally" Walker is an American actress. She made her television debut in the NBC daytime soap Santa Barbara before starring in the shorts True Blue and Moon Over Miami. In the 1990s, Walker starred in films such as Universal Soldier, Singles, When the Bough Breaks, While You Were Sleeping, Kazaam, and Happy, Texas. From 1996 to 1999, she starred as Dr Samantha Waters in the NBC crime drama Profiler, for which she received a Saturn Award nomination for Best TV Actress. She returned to television with a role in the HBO TV series Tell Me You Love Me, and then played the antagonist Agent June Stahl in the FX crime drama Sons of Anarchy. Walker also starred in the 2011 short-lived crime drama The Protector. In seasons 4 and 5 of Longmire, she played the role of Dr. Donna Monaghan, a veteran therapist who is mistress of protagonist.
