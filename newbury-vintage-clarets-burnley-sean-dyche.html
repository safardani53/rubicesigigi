    THE footy season might be over a month away, but owner Alan Tattersall has £200,000 reasons to roar home a winner after 3 o’clock this afternoon.

    A huge Burnley fan, his Vintage Clarets holds a massive chance in today’s Super Sprint at Newbury.

    And his horse could not be in better hands given he’s trained by Richard Fahey, a three time winner of the five furlong dust-up since 2013.

    Even Burnley manager Sean Dyche will be cheering on the sprinter, who is only behind Chipotle in the betting for the big race today.

    Fahey said: “Alan was over the moon with the video message he got from Sean, that was a nice surprise for him. It’s been posted on social media and lots of people have seen it.

    “He’s a massive Burnley fan, but also a big supporter of mine and we’re hoping for another big day together. He told me the other day I’ve had 103 winners for him which is remarkable.
