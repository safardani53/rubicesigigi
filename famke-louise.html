    Famke Louise Career, Professional Life

    In terms of her professional life, Famke Louise launched her own YouTube channel in February 2016. Initially, she made prank videos with several friends; however, several of her videos were criticized as being fake. She decided to delete all of her previous videos in response to the criticism.

    She began working on her singing career at the end of 2017. In November 2017, she released the single “Op Me Monnie.” Following that, she released her singles “Vroom” and “Lit.” In March 2018, she also released the single “Fan,” which she co-wrote with rapper Ronnie Flex.

    She also appeared in the 19th season of the Dutch television series “Expeditie Robinson” in September 2018 and StukTV’s Jachtseizoen in November 2018. Moreover, she has her own documentary series on Videoland, which premiered in November at the Tuschinski cinema in Amsterdam. She has been signed to Ali B’s management office since the end of 2018.

    In 2019, she appeared as a guest jury member on Holland’s Next Top Model and on Ik hou van Holland.

    She also produced a documentary called Bont Girl (translated as Fur Girl) in 2020, in which she exposed the Chinese fur industry.
