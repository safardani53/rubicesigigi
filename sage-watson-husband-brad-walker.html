    Despite not having exchanged vows yet, fans are very curious to discover the identity of the soon-to-be husband of Sage Watson.

    Sage Watson, who specializes in the 400-meter hurdles, was born on June 20, 1994.

    Watson represents Canada internationally and is the reigning 400-meter hurdles Pan American champion.

    In addition, she also won Pan American medals in 2015 and 2019 while competing for Canada’s relay squad.

    Furthermore, the runner is a staunch advocate of mental health. She herself had a phase where she had to battle her own demons.

    Watson credits god for easing the process of her healing experience. 

    The athlete stated that her injury had made her really sad, but spending time on the ranch with her family was a therapeutic experience for her.

    Watson also seeks to help others in their fitness journey as she has her own physical training programs along with nutritional coaching.

    Along with that, Sage is a nature lover. Watson can be seen going on wild adventures, taking the most scenic routes. 

    Brad Walker: Soon-To-Be Husband Of Sage Watson

    The handsome Brad Walker is the soon-to-be husband of Watson, as revealed on their Instagram post. 

    Talking about their relationship, they met for the first time in 2020 in Atlanta, where Sage had to run “fake” track races to meet contract requirements over covid. 

    The couple went on their first date on May 09, 2021, as Brad took her to In-N-Out. He then took her to the beach to watch the sunset.

    Afterward, they went for dinner at Shabu which specializes in Japanese food, as both of them love Japanese food.

    On June 10, 2021, Walker asked Watson to be his girlfriend, and she definitely said yes.

    In the August and September of 2021, they both met each other’s family for the first time.

    On March 29, 2023, Brad finally proposed to Sage on a beach with a romantic picnic that included champagne and red flowers after taking her on a trip to the coast. 

    According to the Knot, the couple is now set to tie the knot on September 24, 2023, in Elkwater, Ab, Canada.

    Regarding Brad’s personal details, he is a wood craftsman by profession. Brad makes a variety of objects by working with wood which ranges from decorative pieces to furniture.

    Back Injury That Led To Miss 2020 Tokyo Olympics

    Just a month and a half remaining before the 2020 Tokyo Olympics, Watson was hit with very sad news. 

    Sage claimed that she had hurt her back despite doing everything to prepare.

    The Olympian said that she entered this year hoping to do better than she did the previous one, but her injury persisted.

    She posted on Instagram saying that she had decided to take the very difficult decision to step back from racing for the 2022 season.

    The main reason was that she could fully recover and give her body the break it needed.

    She is currently continuing her recovery and training journey. As of now, she is focused on the 2024 Olympics. 

    Sage has incorporated a wide range of treatment processes in her recovery regime, including acupuncture therapy.

    We wish her all the very best in her future endeavors!
