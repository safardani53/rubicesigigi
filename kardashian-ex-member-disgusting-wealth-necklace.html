    KYLIE Jenner's ex boyfriend has been slammed by fans for showering his new love with a pricey necklace.

    Rapper Tyga turned to Avril Lavigne after breaking up with the Kardashians star in 2017.

    New York City celebrity jeweler Eric Mavani reportedly created the custom diamod chain.

    It's valued at $80K, according to TMZ.

    The stunning necklace features 50 carats of white diamonds, black diamonds and pink sapphires, the outlet reported.

    The chain also features several pendants, including one with Avril's first name, two with the letter A imposed over a pink heart, and a pair of skulls and crossbones with pink ribbons.

    KYLIE AND TYGA

    Kylie and Tyga first met at Kendall's Sweet 16 birthday party when Kylie was only 14.

    When Kylie turned 17, she celebrated at a star-studded occasion hosted by Tyga in 2014.

    The two officially began dating by the time she was 18 in August 2015.

    What followed was an on-again-off-again romance, with Tyga appearing on several episodes of Keeping Up With the Kardashians in 2015.

    However, the two finally called it quits in April 2017.

    According to Kylie, she and Tyga had an amicable split.

    In an episode of Life of Kylie, she said there "was absolutely not one thing wrong with me and T. He and I will always, always have a bond."

    She added: "There was no crazy fight, we just decided … well, I decided that I'm really young.

    "I don't want to look back in five years from now and feel like he took something from me when he's really not that type of person."

    In April 2017, Kylie began dating Travis Scott.
