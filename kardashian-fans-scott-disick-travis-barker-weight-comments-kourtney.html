    KARDASHIAN fans have compared Kourtney's husband Travis Barker and her ex-partner Scott Disick after she spoke out about her weight gain.

    After watching a recent episode of The Kardashians, viewers were reminded of some nasty comments Scott made to Kourtney about losing her baby weight.

    The reminder came after Kourtney, 43, praised Travis during the third episode of season two as she explained recent changes to her body.

    The Poosh founder revealed she felt insecure after gaining weight while undergoing IVF.

    While on the set of a magazine shoot, she told her assistant Liz Muller and stylist Dani Michelle: “So obviously my body has changed.

    "But it was all of the hormones that the doctors put me on. Eight months of IVF treatment took such a toll on me mentally and physically.

    "Everyone comments in every photo that I’m pregnant. And we wish that and if it’s in God’s plan then it is.”

    Kourtney then talked about how her husband Travis, 46, has encouraged her to have a positive attitude about her body.

    “Travis always tells me every day, ‘You’re perfect,'" she confessed.

    "Like if I complain about any little thing, he’s like, 'You’re perfect, you’re so fine. You’ve never been better.’ So now I’m so into it.

    "My a** is amazing. I’m so into my thicker body. I looked at photos of my body when I was so skinny and it was so cringy.”

    Kourtney's words immediately reminded fans of something Scott once said to her about her weight during their on-off relationship.

    In a scene which aired on Kourtney and Kim Take Miami, Scott urged Kourtney to lose weight just three months after giving birth to their second child, Penelope.

    In the shocking 2013 clip, Scott said to her: "Last time, you definitely tried to watch what you were eating after you had Mason,” referring to their first child who was three-years-old at the time.

    Kourtney replied: "Yeah, last time around I went overboard," to which Scott responded: "At least you were losing weight."

    Clearly annoyed by what he had said, Kourtney told Scott that he was “so crazy” and then explained that she was trying to lose weight "the healthy way".

    But Scott continued and said: "Do it the healthy way, just do it faster."

    When Kourtney questioned why Scott cared so much, he fired back: "Why wouldn’t I? You’re my piece of machinery."

    At the time, Kourtney was understandably upset by what he'd said, and Scott ended up facing a huge backlash online when the episode aired.

    Discussing the comparison between Scott and Travis in the last week, fans described it as "crazy" different.

    “Not that I think Travis is completely unproblematic, but the juxtaposition of his comments to Kourtney gaining some weight vs. Scott’s is crazy,” one said.
