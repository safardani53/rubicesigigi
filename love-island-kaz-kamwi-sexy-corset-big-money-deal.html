    LOVE Island star Kaz Kamwi looked incredible as she starred in her first solo campaign for lingerie giant Boux Avenue.

    The 27-year-old signed a big money deal to front their new party range – and turned up the heat in a sexy corset top.

    The influencer – who is on course to be a millionaire – modelled a series of dresses for the festive season after landing the five-figure pay check.

    Speak about her new gig, an excited Kaz said: “I have worked with Boux Avenue for many years, so to launch my very own edit is simply a  dream come true.

    “My relationship with Boux as a brand is really special as they supported me long before Love Island.

    “Party season is just around the corner so my limited edition dress and  corset collection is just perfect for all things Christmas, glitz and glamour.

    “As a brand partner,  Boux Avenue represent so much more than fashion – their culture of celebrating female  empowerment and body confidence for ALL aligns perfectly with my core values. I hope  everyone loves this edit as much as I do – it’s a real pinch me moment!”

    A source previously told The Sun: “Kaz has worked with Boux Avenue for a long time, but she’s now doing her first solo campaign.

    “It’s a really exciting time for her and just the start of lots very lucrative jobs coming her way. She’ll be a millionaire influencer like Molly-Mae in no time.”

    Since leaving the show over the summer, Kaz has been working along Spotify, Missguided and Revolution make-up to name just a few brands.

    She has also continued to be loved-up with boyfriend Tyler Cruickshank, who she met in the villa.
