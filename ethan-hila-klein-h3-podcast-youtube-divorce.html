    Ethan and Hila Klein: Relationship timeline

    YouTubers Ethan and Hila Klein have been married since 2012.

    However, there have been rumors of a divorce circulating on the internet.

    The first time these rumors came up was in 2019 when the couple made a clickbait video tItled, We Broke Up, where Ethan told his podcast guests that he had broken up with Hila.

    As a result, fans were divided as to whether it was just a trick to get people to click on the video, or if they had really broken up.

    This caused fans to ask the couple to show proof that they were married. In response, Hila then responded by showing their wedding pictures and her wedding ring in their H3H3 Q and A video as evidence.

    Furthermore, a 2019 podcast of the couple saw Hila talking to Ethan about an accusation of some form of cheating because Ethan looked up the Instagram account of a fan that wrote a letter to him saying she like him.

    Rumors began circulating around the internet that same year that there was an impending split because Hila jokingly gave Ethan divorce papers stating that she was divorcing him because she had issues with how he ate food that made him have stomach problems during taste tests.

    Ethan also defended that joke saying they did that for the show because podcast listeners loved that part.

    However, in October 2022, rumors of their divorce began circulating again because someone uploaded a video about Hila and Ethan.

    However, Hila made a post on Instagram about the wedding anniversary to debunk any rumors of a divorce.

    As of the time of writing, Hila and Ethan have not publicly stated that they are divorced.

    Who is Ethan and Hila Klein?

    Ethan and Hila Klein have a YouTube channel named H3H3 productions.

    They have over six million YouTube subscribers and over one million subscribers on their H3 podcast.

    Ethan and Hila met in Israel in 2007 and subsequently tied the knot in October 2012.

    Hila is also the creator and designer of Teddy Fresh, a Los Angeles -based clothing brand.

    After years of dating, Hila and Ethan tied the knot in October 2012.

    In June 2019, they welcomed their first child, a son named Theodore.

    Hila gave birth to her and Ethan's second child, a son named Bruce, in February 2022.
