    Anushri Mane Professional Career

    Anushri began her career as a model and has appeared in numerous fashion shows. In 2018, she finished as the second runner-up in the ‘Miss Teen Satara 2018’ competition. She competes with a lot of young models her age in her twenties. Aside from that, she has a josh app account under the name ‘Anushrisurekhamane,’ where she has 60k fans as of June 2021.

    Mane made her first appearance in the web series in 2019. She played Nilu in the Marathi web series ‘Shala.’ It tells the story of two high school friends. Ashish Shravani directed the film. She had the opportunity to collaborate with Kaushal and Adinath Jadhav.

    In 2020, she was cast as Priyanka Mane in the web series ‘Marathi Shala.’ Anushri appeared in another web series called ‘Tantaar’ in 2021, playing the role of Anjali. It was a Marathi horror and comedy series.

    She has also appeared in a number of music videos. She made her debut in ‘Premachi Dhun,’ alongside Adinath Jadhav. Then she acted in the song ‘Tu Sang Na.’ She recently finished filming the song ‘Love You Bolna.’
