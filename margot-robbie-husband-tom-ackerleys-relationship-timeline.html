    Margot Robbie and husband Tom Ackerley were business partners before they exchanged vows.

    The pair crossed paths while working on 2013’s Suite Française and developed a close friendship before their connection turned romantic. “We were friends for so long,” Robbie told Vogue in 2016. “I was always in love with him, but I thought, ‘Oh, he would never love me back. Don’t make it weird, Margot. Don’t be stupid and tell him that you like him.’ And then it happened, and I was like, ‘Of course, we’re together. This makes so much sense, the way nothing has ever made sense before.'”

    Robbie and Ackerley became business partners, moving to London in 2014 to kickstart LuckyChap Entertainment with several colleagues. The production company has since released several famous films, including the Oscar-nominated Promising Young Woman and the blockbuster Barbie movie. In December 2016, Us Weekly confirmed that the twosome quietly tied the knot in Australia.
