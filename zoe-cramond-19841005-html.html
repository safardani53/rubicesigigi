    Zoe Cramond is a New Zealand actress. After graduating from Unitec Institute of Technology, Cramond appeared in many theatrical productions and TV commercials. She appeared in Outrageous Fortune and Shortland Street, before deciding to give up her acting career to study graphic design. She returned to theaters after being cast for the dramas Panic at Rock Island and Go Girls. In 2011, Cramond joined the cast of the comedy-drama Packed to the Rafters as Emma Mackey, marking her first leading role. She moved to Sydney to shoot. Cramond was in season 12 of the Universe Jump in 2012. She and her dancer Aric Yegudkin came in third place. The following year, Cramond filmed in Fat Tony & Co. From 2015 to 2020, she played the role of Amy Williams in Neighbors.
