    R&B artist Trey Songz cheekily promoted his $20-a-month Only Fans account in light of his alleged sex video scandal.

    According to reports, a nude video of Songz leaked online, however there is no confirmation that it is in fact him as the man's face is not shown.

    In light of the speculation, the Mr. Steal Yo Girl hitmaker took to his Twitter and Instagram to promote his OF account, which he launched in December.

    Taking to his Instagram, he shared a screenshot of his Twitter post, which read: "Going live on OnlyFans now pull up."

    The singer than teasingly wrote alongside the post on his Instagram: "You say they leaked what baby I’m outchea...".

    Yesterday, the artist, whose real name is Tremaine Aldon Neverson, made a cryptic response to sex tape rumors.

    The rapper posted a photograph of him looking at a cellphone on Wednesday afternoon with a shocked emoji as the caption.

    Songz, whose real name is Tremaine Aldon Neverson, is seen wearing an all pink outfit with a cap that's embellished with "h**smad."

    He also shared a link to his track "Don't Judge" on Twitter with a suggestive face emoji.

    His latest post comes after claims surfaced that a naked video of someone, who people claim is Trey, began circulating online.

    In the X-rated clip, an unidentified woman is performing oral sex on a man, who fans allege is Songz.

    However, the man's face is not showing in the video, making it unclear whether or not it is the musician.

    Neverson has not commented directly on the video, or whether it is him in it.

    Several fans on social media allege Songz is the man in the video based on his tattoos matching that of the unidentified man's body while others claimed that the man’s voice in the leaked film is his.

    The Circles singer started trending on Twitter after the rumored sex tape leaked.

    One fan wrote on Twitter, "His music hit different now ... Alexa play 'neighbors know my name by trey songz' ... #treysongz."

    Another fan responded to the video by tweeting, "God has favorites and Trey Songz was one of em."

    While another fan tweeted, "Trey Songz I see why the neighbors knew ya name."

    Another fan took the opportunity to poke fun at Songz recent arrest during a football game between the Kansas City Chiefs and Buffalo Bills on January 24.

    "No wonder Trey Songz walking around slapping police officers," the fan tweeted.
