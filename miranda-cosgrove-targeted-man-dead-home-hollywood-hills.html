    Well, this is one way to start the weekend. Miranda Cosgrove, 23, and the rest of her neighbors in Hollywood Hills got a rude awakening when police officers filled her residence area late on Dec. 17. Officials reportedly found an unidentified man dead in a home only a few houses down from Miranda’s, according to TMZ. Yikes!

    Law enforcement reportedly responded to a call about shots being fired in the area around 11 p.m. on the Friday night. After arriving to the scene, the unknown man was reportedly found with a gun and ammo next to his body, according to reports. He was also engulfed in flames, but the Los Angeles Fire Department quickly resolved it upon their arrival, TMZ reports. Police have not released the identity of the deceased.

    At this time, the incident is reportedly believed to be a suicide. Nobody else is suspected of being involved, according to authorities. Nevertheless, this is still a frightening thing to happen so close to home!

    Aside from the insane incident that went down around her residence, Miranda has been living a pretty chill life. Only two days before the suspected suicide, Miranda shared a glimpse of her early holiday festivities on Instagram. In one image, the former childhood star was captured sitting at a dinner table with a group of her gal pals. “Celebrating another semester down,” she captioned the pic. SO cute! The Drake and Josh alum has reportedly been attending the University of Southern California, where she majors in film.

    Miranda has not made any statement regarding the neighborhood incident. We are really sorry to hear about the scary incident that happened, but it’s good to know that she was not harmed or too badly affected by it.
