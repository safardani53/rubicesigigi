    It lets you turn your iPhone into a speaker - and it’s seriously easy to set up.

    The feature is designed to let you play sound louder than usual.

    CNN Underscored took to Instagram to share the useful hack with their 94,000 followers - and you can benefit too.

    Here’s how it works: go to Settings, Accessibility, Audio and Visual, Headphone Accommodations, and turn this to "strong."

    CNN Underscored said: “Now your phone will play music much louder!”
