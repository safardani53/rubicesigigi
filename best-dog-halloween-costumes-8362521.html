    Best Disney Dog Halloween Costumes at Amazon

    Rubie's Disney Princess Pet Costume Belle, $19 (Save 32%)

    Transform your pup from beast to beauty with Rubie's Belle Disney Princess Pet Costume, fully equipped with a golden gown fans of the classic animated film Beauty and the Beast will immediately recognize.     If Belle isn’t your favorite Disney princess, there are more princess costume options available from Rubie’s Store, including Ariel, Snow White, and Cinderella. As one five-star reviewer noted, “My dog looked like a real princess.”

    Disney for Pets Mickey Mouse Halloween Costume for Dogs, $11 with Coupon

    Other standout Disney costumes include the icon himself, Mickey Mouse, and one of his best friends, Donald Duck, if you’re looking for coordinating costumes for two dogs.     Best Small Dog Halloween Costumes at Amazon

    Star Wars Yoda Halloween Pet Costume, $12 with Coupon

    Dog owners who are fans of the Star Wars franchise won’t want to miss out on the Star Wars Yoda Halloween Pet Costume, marked down with double discounts thanks to a coupon in the product description. The lightweight costume includes Yoda’s cloak and a hood with his instantly recognizable green ears attached. Plus, it’s machine washable. The brand notes that this Star Wars dog costume runs a little small, so size up when ordering.

    DC Comics Pet Costume Wonder Woman, $16 (Save 30%)

    There are tons of small dog Halloween costumes on sale from Amazon, so your pomeranian, pug, beagle, or other little pet can get in on the fun. For DC Comics fans, check out this Wonder Woman costume your little dog would look invincible in. The full outfit is a dress that has a red top with the Wonder Woman symbol, a gold belt, a blue skirt with white stars, and a headpiece.

    “My pup wore this for her Halloween party at doggie daycare. She was a big hit,” one reviewer shared. “It seemed to be comfortable for her, she seemed at ease at having it on. It was very well-made for the price.”

    If you’re dressing up as a vampire this holiday, there’s a matching black and red caped costume to coordinate. Or, if you want your dog’s festive look to match the seasonal decor you may have in your house, dress up your pup in this hooded pumpkin costume. There’s also a green dinosaur suit marked down to $13 that shoppers called “soft and cute” in their reviews.

    Best Large Dog Halloween Costumes at Amazon

    Tomsenn Lion Mane for Dogs, $13 (Save 54%)

    There are plenty of choices for large dog Halloween costumes, too. This lion mane makes for an easy costume, and it’s 54 percent off right now. Your pet can pass for a more ferocious beast thanks to this set that comes with the man, little lion ears, and an additional tailpiece.     One shopper explained that their dog was the center of attention while wearing the costume. “He tolerated it on him for hours while we went out trick or treating… He was approached by at least three dozen people and loved all the attention.”

    If you’re looking for an ironic choice that’s sure to crack up your friends and family, transform your pet into the one person they bark at most with this UPS dog costume.
