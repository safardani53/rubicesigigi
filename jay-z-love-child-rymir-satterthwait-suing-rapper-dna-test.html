    Jay Z Hit With Paternity Suit By Secret Son: New Report

    Could Jay Z be the father of a 21-year-old boy from New Jersey? A new report has surfaced stating that the rapper was involved with a woman in the 1990’s and had a love child, Rymir Satterthwaite, according to The National Enquirer.

    Court documents uncovered in the report show that Jay was sued by Rymir’s guardian, a paralegal named Lillie Coley, in Dec 2014. The rapper is being accused of lying to the court to avoid taking a DNA test, according to the outlet.

    That’s not good news for Jay.

    Prior to Rymir suing Jay, his mother tried taking the rapper to court and get him to submit to a test back in 2010, according to the new report. The case was dismissed by New Jersey Superior Court Judge Edward McBride, Jr., because he found that he did not have jurisdiction, according to the outlet. At the time, Jay Z’s lawyer argued that Hova lives in Manhattan, not New Jersey.

    Wow, this has been going on for years!

    But Rymir’s guardian is claiming Jay lied, because the rapper reportedly owns three houses in the Garden State. She is claiming Jay’s lawyer gave “fraudulent” and “false” information to a judge.

    It’s going to be very interesting to see how this all plays out for the rapper.

    This Isn’t The First Scandal For Jay Z

    This isn’t the first love child scandal for Jay.

    Previously it was alleged that the rapper was the father of a young boy with model Shenelle Scott. Again, Jay Z has never admitted to it, according to the outlet. He reportedly paid the woman off with a whooping $1 million, but in return she had to keep his name off the birth certificate.

    Wow, we wonder if Hova will take the same route with Rymir. If the report is true, hopefully Jay will do what’s right for his son.
