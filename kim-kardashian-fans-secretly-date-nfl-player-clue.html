    KIM Kardashian has sparked speculation that her next boyfriend could be a hunky NFL player.

    Kim, 41, attended the Los Angeles Rams vs. Dallas Cowboys football game at SoFi Stadium on Sunday with her six-year-old son Saint and posted some videos on her Instagram Story.

    The first image was a picture of Kim's small metallic blue Balenciaga handbag positioned next to some Rams merchandise including a foam finger.

    This particular snap set tongues wagging after one fan reposted it on Reddit and wrote alongside it: "Ok, this is WILD WILD speculation. But they rarely post anything for no reason…could Kim’s next beau be a Ram? (This specific player seems closest in age and looks to who Kim would go for)."

    The player in question was 32-year-old Bobby Wagner who is a middle linebacker for the Los Angeles Rams.

    Fans immediately commented on the post and were divided about the idea of Kim dating Bobby.

    Someone else suggested: "Plot twist: she’s buying the Rams," to which one Reddit user replied: "Now that wouldn’t surprise me at this point."

    It's unsure what Bobby would make of the fact that mom-of-four Kim was booed at the football game after she appeared on the jumbotron.

    Wearing an all black spandex outfit and oversized black sunglasses, both by Balenciaga, the Skims boss sat down to watch the game with her son Saint.

    As the camera showed Kim in the crowd, she waved and blew the 70,000 spectators a kiss.

    But the controversial Hulu star suffered a frosty reception, as loud boos echoed around the SoFi.

    On Thursday, he sat down with Fox News’ Tucker Carlson and slammed his ex wife for using “sexualized” imagery with her brand Skims, and claimed that her designs were “based off ideas" from his fashion label Yeezy.
