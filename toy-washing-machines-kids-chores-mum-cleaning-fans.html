    MUMS are raving about miniature toy washing machines for their kids which is getting them interested in doing chores. 

    Parents are teaching their children about laundry from an early age with the mini machines from Argos, with some mums saying they're just like the real thing.

    The tiny machines are being sold by the homeware store for just £28, and they’ve got hundreds of glowing reviews. 

    The toy washing machines, standing just under a foot tall, come in a classic white-and-grey combo or a bright pink model. 

    Mums are gushing over the realistic functions and accessories, including a mini washing basket and soap powder box.
