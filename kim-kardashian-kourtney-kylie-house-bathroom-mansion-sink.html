    Inside the Kardashians' WILD bathrooms featuring Kourtney's $3K two-person tub and Kim's futuristic

    The KARDASHIANS' custom-designed mansions worth millions can be divisive, but they certainly put the lavish in lavatory.

    From Kourtney’s $3K two-person bathtub to Kim’s futuristic “flat sink,” here’s a tour of their WILD bathrooms.

    KOURTNEY'S $8M HOME & TUB FOR TWO

    Kourtney Kardashian, 42, resides in an $8M Calabasas LA mansion which she bought back in 2014 from retired football player Keyshawn Johnson.

    She once shared the home with ex Scott Disick, with whom she also shares kids Mason, 12, Penelope, nine, and Reign, seven.

    She's since moved on with Blink-182 rocker Travis Barker and fans speculate Kourtney could announce baby number four with her fiancé any day.

    Although the star recently purchased a $12M Palm Springs stunner and also joked about moving to Napa, upsetting mom Kris Jenner, the POOSH founder has it posh already.

    Her 11,500-square-foot Tuscan-style abode features a massive lounge-style main bathroom that is often splashed across her Instagram page.

    Its centerpiece is its two-person freestanding lily-white tub - a comparable model starts at $2.6K.

    The room is also decorated with a wooden stool for dipping in and out, priced around $300, and a hand-knotted rug, per AD, that costs $6K for a similar style.

    Kourtney, meanwhile, recently posted to Instagram Stories while relaxing in a tub watching singer Alanis Morrisette on a huge mounted TV.

    The KUWTK star captured her video: "UGH OBSESSED."

    Based on the all-black hardware, it appeared to be a different bathroom, but since her home has NINE, the TV tub could simply be down the hall.

    KIM'S $60M HOME & 'FLAT SINK'

    Kim, 41, purchased her $60M Hidden Hills complex with ex-husband Kanye West for $20M, with whom she shares kids North, eight; Saint, six; Chicago, four, and Psalm, two, and a seemingly endless feud.

    Also seemingly endless is her futuristic fortress, which cost five years and $40M to renovate with Kim once describing it as a "money pit."

    In 2020, the ex-couple sat down to share the inspirations behind their wildly minimalistic and neutral-toned creation - often mocked by fans as "morgue like" - with Architectural Digest.

    "I've never used our pool actually," Kim admitted on the amenity that cost $50K alone during the memorable video, which definitely revealed some cracks in the facade.

    That being said, the Donda is in the details.

    Kim first gave fans a glimpse at her primary bathroom which has huge open windows in a massive space via her Instagram stories in 2019, including a "light box" ceiling and her uber-futuristic “flat sink.”

    Created by Kanye alongside two acclaimed designers, Kim acknowledged that people were “a little confused” by the device as she toured the room.

    The faucet sits atop a slab of stone with one open slit, yet the slab does “slightly slope down with a slit for the water," and the water can be as "high pressured as you like with no backsplash."

    “Eight versions of this prototype sink were made,” Kim added.

    Fans were stunned by the futuristic invention even if the excessive "flaunting" of her wealth was hard to let 'sink' in.

    “Her bathroom is literally the size of my house like I’m not kidding it is LITERALLY the size of my house,” one fan wrote.

    KYLIE'S OFFICE SHOWER SPLASHBACK

    Kylie Jenner, 26, may not be a "billionaire" but sold half her beauty brand in 2019 pocketing $340M.

    She shares two children with Travis Scott and while Kylie makes the latrine list, it's for a rather cold reason.

    She was slammed by fans for the personal shower at her Kylie Cosmetics office in LA - and it's "weak" water pressure.

    Kylie recently gave fans a glimpse of her walk-in shower on her Instagram Stories last month, leaving followers surprised by the water pressure and basic showerhead.

    One tweeted: "Why is nobody talking about how s**t Kylie Jenner’s shower is?

    "The water pressure AND the size of the shower head. Someone get that gal a plumber pronto."

    Another, believing the shower to be in Kylie's Holmsby Hills complex, wrote: "Kylie Jenner lives in a $35m mansion, and this is the water pressure..."

    Kylie - who was recently named the highest-paid celebrity of 2020 - does have multiple mansions in LA.

    Last April, she took a $36M dip on a seven-bathroom compound in Holmsby Hills with a home theater, multiple bars and games rooms, a gym and a basketball court.
